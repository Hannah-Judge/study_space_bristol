About

As well as serving as a (hopefully) useful tool for students who like to work in cafes, Study Space: Bristol is also a creative platform for students/recent graduates who identify as women, or have complex identities that includes woman. Our team includes coders, writers, photographers, and graphic designers. We are always looking to grow our team. If you are interested in joining us, please get in touch via the form below.
